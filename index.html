Welcome to Our Campus

Located just 3 km from NH-37 and 25 km from Nagaon, Assam, our institution provides a serene environment perfect for academic excellence. We offer comprehensive infrastructure including modern classrooms, well-equipped libraries, advanced laboratories, and comfortable hostel facilities.

Affiliated with Srimanta Sankaradeva University of Health Sciences and approved by the Pharmacy Council of India, we are committed to nurturing future leaders in pharmaceutical science.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We pride ourselves on providing world-class pharmaceutical education through experienced faculty, modern facilities, and industry partnerships. Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while addressing local healthcare needs.

With a focus on practical learning, research opportunities, and professional development, we prepare our students for successful careers in the pharmaceutical industry and healthcare sector.

Founder

Mr. Nurul Huda

Mr. Nurul Huda, the visionary Founder of the Huda Group of Institutions, laid the foundation of the organization with the mission of providing quality and affordable education to all sections of society. His unwavering commitment to academic excellence and social upliftment continues to inspire the institution's growth and achievements.

Under his leadership, the Minority Welfare Educational Society was established in 1991, with a focus on promoting education as a means of empowerment. His forward-thinking approach and deep concern for students' holistic development have shaped the institution's strong academic and ethical foundation.

Huda Group of Institutions: Founded in 2014

Principal

Dr. Manoj Kumar Kakati

Dr. Manoj Kumar Kakati has completed his B.Pharm and M.Pharm from PES College of Pharmacy, Bangalore, affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Karnataka. He has served as a Research Scientist in a reputed organization and has held key academic positions, including Head of the Department (HOD) of Pharmaceutics and Principal at leading pharmacy institutions.

With over 15 years of experience in research and teaching, Dr. Kakati has contributed significantly to the field of pharmaceutical sciences. He has also authored and co-authored various research publications in national and international journals
